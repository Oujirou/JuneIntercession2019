Transfers to CSU/UC

Calfornia State Univerisity General Education Worksheets - Only works for CSU Schools

Having the requirements filled out on these worksheets will allow you to skip your General Education courses you would be taking at any Calfornia State Univerisity location. Requires 39-40 credit hours of classes you could take at Community College. Most colleges do not allow more than 60 credit hours to transfer to a 4 year college. A little less work than the IGETC and no foreign language requirements.

  • College of San Mateo
  • Canada College
  • Skyline College

Intersegmental General Education Transfer Curriculum (IGETC) Worksheet

Having the requirements filled out on these worksheets will allow you to skip your General Education courses you would be taking at a Calfornia State Univerisity or Univerisity of California location. Requires 34-37 (39-42 for UC) credit hours of classes you could take at Community College. Most colleges do not allow more than 60 credit hours to transfer to a 4 year college. You automatically qualify for Arizona State University if you complete your IGETC, which is all online and provides Bachelors Degrees.

  • College of San Mateo
  • Canada College
  • Skyline College
