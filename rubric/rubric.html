Year In Review: 30 Points

For full credit on this assignment you should have more than one page with CSS on all of your elements. You must be able to navigate between each page.

- Relevant Content: 15 Points

- Page Uploaded and Online: 5 Points

- CSS on all Elements: 5 Points

- Daily Commitment: 5 Points

Team Planning: 10 Points

For this assignment each team will make a plan of what your group website will be and who will be responsible for what portions. At the end of the day you should have a breakdown of every page you will need, and who will be responsible for each section. You should plan for 2.5 days worth of work, with 3 hours considered a day.

- Contributed to Planning: 5 Points

- Signed up for Tasks: 5 Points

- Paper/Digital Layout of Required Pages/CSS/Javascript: 5 Points

- Professionalism: 5 Points

Group Project: 40 Points

This will be the actual work done to build your project. Each team is expected to perform a daily standup and select which task you will do. These tasks should be the ones you generated during your planning phase the previous week. Each team will send daily updates to their commitments as well as an end of day status update with what each individual accomplished. The final product should have all pages working and accessable. No broken CSS or 404s, and hosted online.

- Daily Checkins: 15 Points

- Professionalism: 15 Points

- Completed Product: 5 Points

- Presentation: 5 Points

College Worksheet: 10 Points

- Potential College Application List: 10 Points

Final Presentation: 10 Points

Each group with present their finished work, nothing should be broken during the presentation.
